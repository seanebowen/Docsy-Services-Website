Booking Policy
Interpreter services are available on demand — no advance notice required.
Language must be specified at the time of service.
Interpreter fees are collected upfront along with the base service fee.
If Language Line is unable to fulfill the requested language, the interpreter fee will be refunded in full. The base service fee remains non-refundable if the appointment proceeds without interpretation.
Terms & Conditions
Availability
Interpreter services are facilitated through Language Line Solutions, a third-party provider. Docsy Services will make every reasonable effort to connect clients with the requested language. In rare cases, availability cannot be guaranteed for highly specialized or low-demand languages.
No Refunds for Unused Block Time
Interpreter services are sold in fixed increments of 15, 30, or 60 minutes. Once a block is purchased, the full block rate applies regardless of actual usage. If your session concludes before the purchased block expires, no refund or credit is issued for the remaining time. Clients are encouraged to select the increment that best matches their anticipated session length. If a session exceeds the purchased block, additional 15-minute increments will be billed at the applicable tier rate and invoiced immediately following the appointment.
Cancellations
Cancelled before service begins: full interpreter fee refunded
No-show or same-day cancellation after interpreter has been connected: interpreter fee forfeited
Base service cancellation policy applies separately
Scope of Service
Language Line interpreters are provided solely to facilitate communication during the notarial, signing, or reporting session. Docsy Services is not responsible for the accuracy, completeness, or legal sufficiency of any interpretation provided by a third-party interpreter.
Client Responsibility
It is the client's responsibility to accurately identify the language needed. If an incorrect language is requested and the session cannot proceed as a result, the interpreter fee is non-refundable.
